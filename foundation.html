About the Foundation

The Mandela Legacy Foundation is dedicated to creating pathways of opportunity, equity, and empowerment across South Africa and beyond. Rooted in the values of our icons, the Foundation works to close socio-economic divides by championing education, inclusion, and sustainable development.

Our initiatives address both immediate needs and long-term transformation, equipping communities with tools to thrive in the modern economy. Through strategic partnerships and impact-driven programs, we support job creation, skills development, and financial empowerment that shape the future of generations to come.

Our Work – The 4 Pillars

Four strategic focus areas that drive our mission forward and create lasting impact in communities

Workforce Evolution

Our programs prepare the workforce for the future by promoting digital adoption, remote work readiness, and industry-relevant training that meet global standards.

Gender Divide

The Foundation actively tackles systemic inequalities, ensuring that both men and women benefit equally from opportunities in education, employment, and leadership.

Smart Money

Beyond financial basics, we teach smart money habits that emphasize entrepreneurship, investment readiness, and wealth creation for long-term security.

Skills Acceleration

We accelerate learning in high-demand skills, including technology, business services, and creative industries, enabling youth to compete in the global economy.

Board of Advisors

The Mandela Legacy Foundation is guided by a diverse Board of Advisors who bring expertise across business, academia, policy, and social impact. They ensure that our work remains aligned with both local community needs and international best practices.

The Board provides oversight, strategic guidance, and mentorship to ensure all initiatives deliver measurable, sustainable impact. Their support allows the Foundation to expand its reach, strengthen partnerships, and maintain accountability in driving social and economic transformation.
